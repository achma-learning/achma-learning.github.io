Daily Program - Internalize and Master

  • Sleep-Wake Mastery: Sleep by 23:00 or 00:00, rise at 05:00 or 06:00. This routine strengthens your body and mind.
    I control my time and energy. Each day begins with clarity.
  • Mental Clarity and Focus: Set goals, walk for 15 minutes, visualize success. Your focus sharpens with each step.
    My focus is strong, my vision guides me forward.
  • Deep Work Immersion: Dedicate 4 hours to focused work. Eliminate distractions. Each task builds mastery.
    I am immersed in my work, growing every moment.
  • Physical Discipline: Perform 50 push-ups and squats. Strength in the body mirrors strength in the mind.
    My body is strong, my mind is disciplined.
  • Skill and Mastery: Spend 20 minutes to 1 hour daily learning, or review with Anki. Every day you grow closer to mastery.
    Each day I learn, I grow in skill and competence.
